Admissions

Admission Process

  1. Collect an application form from the school office in Thika.
  2. Submit the form with a copy of the student's Birth Certificate.
  3. The student will undergo an interview or entry assessment.
  4. Upon successful assessment, pick up the admission letter and full fee structure.
