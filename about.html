About Our School

Our History

Founded under the auspices of the Anglican Church of Kenya (ACK), Bishop Njuguna Academy began with a vision to provide quality, affordable, and value-based education to the Thika community.

Over the years, we have grown from a small primary school into a leading Academy and Junior Secondary School, consistently producing students who excel both in character and academic performance.

Our Mission

To provide a conducive environment for holistic growth through quality education and Christian values, ensuring every child reaches their full potential.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in nurturing disciplined, innovative, and God-fearing global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Core Values

Faith in God, Integrity, Excellence, and Teamwork. These pillars guide our students and staff in everything we do.

Headteacher's Message

"Welcome to ACK Bishop Njuguna Academy. We believe every child is a unique gift from God. Our goal is to unlock their potential not just academically, but socially and spiritually. By combining the CBC curriculum with strong Christian foundations, we prepare our learners for a bright and purposeful future."

— Mrs. Jane Kamau, Headteacher
